About Me

My name is Joy Curth. I'm IT Manager and an entrepreneur. I love to travel and am passionate about wellness. I love the mantras of follow your bliss and do more of what makes you happy. After becoming a yoga and pilates teacher, aromatherapist, herbalist and Ayurvedic consultant, I have learned the power of how we can heal ourselves holistically. There are so many modalities we can do this with ,such as reiki and flower essences too. I expecially love combining all of them for self-healing with technology. I grew up in NH but left 8 years ago to move to Sydney, Australia. From there I moved to San Diego for 4 years, but have just relocated back to the Seacoast for a business opportunity to open a healing tea and apothecary shop in Portsmouth, NH.
